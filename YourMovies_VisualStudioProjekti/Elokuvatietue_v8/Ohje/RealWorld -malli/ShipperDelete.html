Shipping > Deleting a shipper

Glossary Item Box

  1. From the Tools menu, click Shipping
  2. Click the Delete button.
  3. Answer Yes to the confirmation prompt to remove the record.
  4. Click OK to save your changes

 

Please note that removing a shipper will not update any orders linked to this shipper.
